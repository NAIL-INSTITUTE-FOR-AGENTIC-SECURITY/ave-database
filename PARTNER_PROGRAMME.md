# NAIL Institute Partner Programme

## Mission

The NAIL Institute Partner Programme connects security researchers,
AI framework vendors, and enterprise teams to collectively strengthen
the agentic AI ecosystem. Partners gain early access to AVE research,
contribute to the vulnerability database, and integrate NAIL tooling
into their products.

---

## Partner Tiers

### 🟢 Community Partner (Free)

**For**: Independent researchers, small teams, open-source projects

**Benefits**:
- Access to the full AVE database and API
- Community Slack/Discord channel
- Attribution in contributed AVE cards
- NAIL Institute "Community Partner" badge
- RSS/Atom feed for new vulnerability alerts

**Requirements**:
- Agree to the [Responsible Disclosure Policy](RESPONSIBLE_DISCLOSURE.md)
- Public attribution for AVE data usage (CC-BY-SA-4.0)

**How to Join**: Open a [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/NAIL-INSTITUTE-FOR-AGENTIC-SECURITY/ave-database/discussions) or email `partners@nailinstitute.org`

---

### 🔵 Research Partner

**For**: Academic institutions, security research labs, AI safety organisations

**Benefits**:
- Everything in Community tier
- Early access to pre-publication AVE cards (30-day embargo)
- Co-authorship opportunities on research papers
- Quarterly research briefings
- Access to quantum computing research collaboration
- Priority review for contributed AVE cards
- NAIL Institute "Research Partner" badge

**Requirements**:
- Active publication record in AI safety, security, or alignment
- Commitment to contribute ≥2 AVE cards per year
- Participation in quarterly research reviews
- Responsible disclosure adherence

**How to Join**: Email `research@nailinstitute.org` with:
1. Organisation name and research focus
2. Relevant publications (last 2 years)
3. Proposed AVE contribution areas

---

### 🟣 Technology Partner

**For**: AI framework vendors, cloud providers, security tooling companies

**Benefits**:
- Everything in Research tier
- Framework-specific hardening guide co-development
- Pre-release access to VS Code extension and GitHub Action
- AI-SBOM integration support
- Joint webinars and content collaboration
- Dedicated engineering contact at NAIL
- "AVE-Compatible" certification for products
- NAIL Institute "Technology Partner" badge

**Requirements**:
- Integration of AVE awareness into product documentation
- Framework-specific hardening guide contribution
- Annual security audit against AVE database
- Public acknowledgement of partnership

**How to Join**: Email `partners@nailinstitute.org` with:
1. Company name and product description
2. Current AI safety/security posture
3. Proposed integration plan
4. Engineering contact details

---

### 🟡 Enterprise Partner

**For**: Enterprises deploying agentic AI in production

**Benefits**:
- Everything in Technology tier
- Private AVE threat briefings (monthly)
- Custom AI-SBOM generation for enterprise stack
- Enterprise CTF challenge packs for team training
- Priority incident response support
- Custom framework hardening assessments
- Executive briefing materials
- Governance and compliance mapping (SOC 2, ISO 27001, NIST AI RMF)
- NAIL Institute "Enterprise Partner" badge

**Requirements**:
- Annual partnership agreement
- Anonymised incident data sharing (optional, encouraged)
- Participation in enterprise advisory board
- Internal AVE awareness training (NAIL-provided materials)

**How to Join**: Email `enterprise@nailinstitute.org` with:
1. Company name and industry
2. Current agentic AI deployment scale
3. Security team size and maturity
4. Specific areas of interest

## Certification Programme

### AVE-Aware Certification

Products and platforms can earn "AVE-Aware" certification by
demonstrating coverage of AVE vulnerability categories:

| Level | Requirement | Badge |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| **Bronze** | Address ≥30% of AVE categories in documentation | 🥉 AVE-Aware |
| **Silver** | Address ≥60% of AVE categories with code mitigations | 🥈 AVE-Hardened |
| **Gold** | Address ≥90% of AVE categories with automated testing | 🥇 AVE-Certified |

### Certification Process

1. **Self-Assessment**: Map product features to AVE categories
2. **Evidence Submission**: Provide documentation/code/test evidence
3. **NAIL Review**: Technical review by NAIL Institute team
4. **Certification**: Badge granted for 12 months (renewable)

## Responsible Disclosure Policy

See [RESPONSIBLE_DISCLOSURE.md](RESPONSIBLE_DISCLOSURE.md) for our
coordinated vulnerability disclosure process.

**Key Points**:
- 90-day disclosure window for new vulnerabilities
- Coordinated disclosure with affected vendors
- Credit given to all contributing researchers
- No legal action against good-faith security research
