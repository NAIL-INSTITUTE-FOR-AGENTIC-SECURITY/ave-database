# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of the AVE Database pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are building the safety infrastructure for agentic AI — a mission that requires trust, rigour, and good-faith collaboration.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behaviour

- **Rigorous good-faith reporting** — Submit what you genuinely believe to be real vulnerabilities
- **Evidence-based discourse** — Back claims with data, logs, or reproducible steps
- **Responsible disclosure** — Do not publish active exploits targeting live production systems
- **Respectful collaboration** — Disagree on severity ratings or categories constructively
- **Credit original work** — Cite prior art and acknowledge related findings

### Unacceptable Behaviour

- Submitting fabricated vulnerabilities or falsified evidence
- Using the AVE Database to coordinate attacks on production systems
- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks against other contributors
- Publishing private disclosure submissions without authorisation
- Weaponising AVE data against specific organisations without coordinated disclosure

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all AVE Database spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any affiliated communication channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to **conduct@nailinstitute.org**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

### Enforcement Actions

1. **Warning** — Private written notice with explanation
2. **Temporary Ban** — Temporary suspension from contributing
3. **Permanent Ban** — Permanent removal from the project
