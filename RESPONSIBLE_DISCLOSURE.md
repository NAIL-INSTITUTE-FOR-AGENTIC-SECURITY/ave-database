# Responsible Disclosure Policy

## Scope

This policy applies to vulnerabilities discovered in:
- Agentic AI systems and frameworks
- The AVE database itself
- NAIL Institute tools, APIs, and infrastructure
- Multi-agent architectures and protocols (e.g., MCP, A2A)

## Reporting

### For Agentic AI Vulnerabilities (New AVE Cards)

1. **Email** security@nailinstitute.org with:
   - Vulnerability description and mechanism
   - Affected frameworks/systems
   - Proof of concept (if available)
   - Proposed severity and category
   - Your preferred attribution name

2. **OR** open a confidential GitHub Security Advisory:
   https://github.com/NAIL-INSTITUTE-FOR-AGENTIC-SECURITY/ave-database/security/advisories

### For NAIL Infrastructure Vulnerabilities

Email security@nailinstitute.org directly. Do **not** open public issues.

## Timeline

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| 0 | Report received; acknowledgement within 48 hours |
| 7 | Initial triage and severity assessment shared with reporter |
| 14 | Affected vendor(s) notified (if applicable) |
| 30 | Draft AVE card shared with reporter for review |
| 60 | Vendor mitigation deadline |
| 90 | Public disclosure via AVE database publication |

Extensions may be granted for complex vulnerabilities requiring
coordinated multi-vendor response.

## Safe Harbour

The NAIL Institute commits to:

- **No legal action** against researchers acting in good faith
- **No penalties** for accessing or testing systems within scope
- **Credit and attribution** for all contributing researchers
- **Coordinated disclosure** respecting vendor remediation timelines

Good faith includes:
- Making reasonable efforts to avoid privacy violations
- Not exploiting vulnerabilities beyond proof of concept
- Not accessing, modifying, or deleting data beyond minimum necessary
- Reporting promptly after discovery

## Recognition

Contributing researchers receive:
- Named attribution on the published AVE card
- Listing on the NAIL Institute Contributors page
- Invitation to the Community Partner tier
- Annual "Top Contributor" recognition
